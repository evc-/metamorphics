The Story

meta meaning about (it's own category).
morph meaning form.
metamorphasis - transformation
metamorphic rocks are the results of transformations of existing rocks through heat and presure.

Metamorphics is the solution to every rockhound's most persistent problem. Once you devise the perfect display for your collection, you're limited by it. Everything has it's place, so there's no room for any more pieces! Metamorphics allows you to keep growing your collection by adding more modular boxes. They come in a variety of sizes to accomodate the diversity of sizes and shapes of your specimens. The magnets on each side means the boxes click nicely into place, and your display can be easily rearranged and transformed. Each box fits perfectly with all other models, meaning there is no limit to the different configuations your collection can take!
